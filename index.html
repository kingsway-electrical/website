Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what people usually ask.

Where is Kingsway Electrical based?

Kingsway Electrical Contractors Ltd is an electrician based in Tunbridge Wells, Kent. We cover Tunbridge Wells and the surrounding areas across Kent, London and East Sussex.

What areas do you cover?

We work throughout Tunbridge Wells, Kent, London and East Sussex, for both domestic and commercial customers.

Are you a qualified and registered electrician?

Yes. We are NAPIT-registered and qualified to NVQ Level 3, 18th Edition, AM2 and City & Guilds 2391 (Inspection and Testing). Every job is certified.

Do you do both domestic and commercial work?

Yes. We handle everything from a single socket in a home to a full commercial fit-out, including lighting, consumer units, rewires, power and distribution boards.

Do you install EV chargers?

Yes, we install home and workplace EV chargers, along with the supply and circuit protection they need.

Do you carry out EICRs and electrical testing?

Yes. We carry out EICRs, certification and fault finding for homes and businesses.

How do I get a quote?

Call or email us, or fill in the contact form on this site with a few details about the job. We can call or email you back, whichever suits.
